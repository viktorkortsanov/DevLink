When you use DevLink, we collect the following types of information:

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following cases:

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information, including:

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Analyze platform usage
  • Improve our services

You can control cookie settings in your browser.

DevLink integrates with third-party services including:

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You can delete your account at any time, and we will remove your data according to our retention policies.
